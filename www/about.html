About

Multimedia installation Explore John Muir's Yosemite illuminates the writings of naturalist John Muir through interactive photography and music, offering an engaging new interpretation of Muir's vision of nature.

The web and iPad versions of Explore John Muir's Yosemite were launched in July 2014. If you have any questions or suggestions, please email nell@nellshawcohen.com.
